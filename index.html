My Biography

I was born in Whittier, California in 1968. My family moved to Fresno around 1975, and I grew up here. After graduating from high school, I attended U.C. Santa Cruz, where I studied Russian, but I did not complete a degree there. I worked as regional manager for Alamo Rent-a-Car from 1991 to 2001, then as an analyst for Fresno County from 2001 to 2006, then I was self-employed as a real estate broker from 2006 to 2010. I went back to school at Fresno City College where I earned an A.S. in Business Administration and an A.S. in Computer Information Technology, both in 2015. I am currently a student at Fresno State, majoring in Information Systems. Just for fun, here are some links to my favorite websites from the 1990s.
